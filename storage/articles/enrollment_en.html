Target of enrollment

Study abroad in high school: After finishing the high school graduation examination, you can apply to study abroad at university. In your senior year, you can study abroad for preparatory courses or apply to study abroad at university after graduating from senior year.

Undergraduate study abroad: ☆Undergraduate courses are four years long. You need to study for one year of preparatory course
☆3+1+1 for undergraduate study. 3+1+1 for undergraduate and master's degree studies

Master's degree: Bachelor degree required, you can apply for a master's degree. There is a 3+1+1 undergraduate and master's degree program.

Master's degree: 1-2 years including preparatory course, 2-3 years including preparatory course

There are differences between schools, please inquire about the project for details.

Doctoral study abroad: Master's degree certificate required: You can apply for doctoral study

Doctoral program duration: 3-5 years including preparatory course

The requirements vary from major to major, please inquire for details.
